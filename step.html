Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. 1. Connect your 9v battery to the positive and negative
    rails at the bottom of the breadboard and the speaker
    to the top rails. Then, connect your transistors C, B,
    and E pins to the 13, 14, and 15 columns respectively.

  2. 2. Connect your resistor to the E and B pins of the transistor.

  3. 3. Connect the negative pin of your capacitor to the B pin of
    the transistor and jump the positive pin of the capacitor
    to column 1.

  4. 4. Jump the bottom negative rail to the C pin of the transistor,
    and jump it to column 2.

  5. 5. Jump pin E of transistor to the top negative rail. Then,
    connect the bottom positive rail to the top positive rail.

  6. 6. Connect the aux signal wire to column 1, and the aux
    ground wire to column 2.
